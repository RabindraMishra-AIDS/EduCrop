FAQ's

What is Educrop ?

Educrop is an Agritech Responsive Website which is developed under the Course RBL.
Educrop Basically Means Education + Crop.

What do you mean by RBL?

RBL means (Research based Learning). Under this course we have to develop our Projects based on Research and Learnings from our Btech. Where in Semester-IV and Semester-V we have to do case studies and write Research Papers.

What is the Main Purpose of Educrop?

Main Objective of Educrop is to Predict the crop Yield based on Datasets and Various Machine Learning Models.
Instead of Using Traditional Methods Educrop Provides Farmers to use Modernistic Approach to determine their Yield of crops and Hence Be ready for the outcomes and results.

What is Machine Learning ?

Machine Learning(ML) can be explained as automating and improving the learning process of computers based on their experiences without being actually programmed.
